Kodiak Grizzly Bear

Grizzly Bears can run faster than 30MPH for at least 2 miles straight.

  • Scientific Name: Ursus Arctos Middendorffi
  • Average Weight:600-1400 lbs
  • Located:Kodiak Archipelago
  • Average Lifespan:25 Years

The Kodiak bear (Ursus arctos middendorffi) inhabits the islands of the Kodiak Archipelago in southwest Alaska. Its Alutiiq name is taquka-aq.It is the largest recognized subspecies of brown bear, and one of the two largest bears alive today, the other being the polar bear.
